complex(v, i) → {complex}

Returns a complex number constructed from either zero, one or two arguments. If no arguments are given, returns 0j. If one argument is given, it is converted to a complex number and returned. If two arguments are given, they are interpreted as the real and imaginary parts of a complex number, respectively.
Parameters:
